# Exercises Database

## Bicep Curl (`bicep_curl_001`)
- **Body Part:** Arms
- **Equipment:** Dumbbell
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Instructions:**
  1. Stand holding dumbbells at your sides.
  2. Curl the weights up towards your shoulders.
  3. Lower them back down slowly.
